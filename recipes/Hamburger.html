Hamburger

This recipe will go over on how to make a basic hamburger. The recipe is easy to make at home. It does not take more than 10 minutes.

Ingredient lists

  • Hamburger buns
  • hamburger patties
  • cheese
  • ketchup/ mustard

Instructions

  1. Preheat pan on stove to medium heat
  2. place hamburger patty on pan
  3. place add seasoning to patty once on stove
  4. Let it cook for about 5 minutes
  5. After 6 minutes, flip burger and let it cook for another 2 minutes. Add cheese if you want some.
  6. place cooked patty on hamburger bottom bun. Add ketchup/mustard if desired
