Carne Asada fries

This recipe will discuss on to make carne asada fries. This recipe is easy to make and does not take a lot of time. While the food is cooking you can prepare the toppings and sauces on the side.

Ingredient lists

  • fries
  • steak
  • Guacamole
  • sour cream
  • shredded cheese

Instructions

  1. Air fry or deep fry any brand of french fries ()
  2. Cook chopped steak while fries are being cooked
  3. Once both have finished cooking, pour fries into a plate
  4. Then add the cooked chopped steak on top
  5. Add any kind of cheese on top of the carne asada fries
  6. Add Guacamole and/or sour cream on top or any desired toppings or sauce
