Sandwich

This recipe will discuss on to make a Sandwhich. This recipe is easy to make and does not take a lot of time. While the food is cooking you can prepare the toppings and sauces on the side.

Ingredient Lists

  • Preffered Sandhich bread
  • choice of meat
  • mayonnaise (optional)
  • cheese (optional)
  • vegetables (optional)

Instructions

  1. Lay one slice of sanwich bread on a clean surface
  2. Place choice of cooked meat on the bread
  3. Apply cheese if wanted
  4. Apply choice of vegetables on top
  5. Apply choice of dressing on other slice Sandwich bread
  6. Place side of dressing on top of the Sandwich to avoid dressing on fingers when holding complete Sandwhich
  7. Enjoy
